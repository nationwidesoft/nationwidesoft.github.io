Lucy's Nightmare

A psychological horror platformer

A fairy tale with two faces. Lucy is trapped in a nightmare she cannot wake from, searching for the self she has lost. You'll leap through whimsical, monster-filled stages — but the moments where her truth surfaces wear an entirely different face.

Scattered diamonds hold the fragments of a reality she has forgotten. Gather them, and a carefully woven story peels the fairy tale back, one layer at a time — until the truth beneath proves crueler than any nightmare.
